# Grade 3 Spelling Exams 7–10

Each exam has:

* 20 unique spelling questions
* 30 seconds per question
* 4 options per question
* 1 correct answer
* Hard/confusing distractors
* Mostly meaning-based and sentence-completion prompts

---
